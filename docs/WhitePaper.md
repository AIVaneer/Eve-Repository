# White Paper

## Introduction
The PCVR capsule represents a significant advancement in the field of virtual reality, enabling fully immersive experiences by leveraging cutting-edge technology.

## Technical Overview
This section delves into the technical specifications and functionalities of the PCVR capsule, including its hardware components, software architecture, and integration capabilities within virtual environments.

### Hardware Specifications
- **Processor:** Custom high-performance chipset
- **Display:** 4K resolution per eye with 120Hz refresh rate
- **Sensors:** 6DoF tracking with depth sensors

### Software Architecture
- **Operating System:** Customized VR OS for optimal performance
- **APIs:** Compatibility with major VR development frameworks (e.g., Unity, Unreal Engine)

### Integration Capabilities
- Support for both standalone and tethered operation
- Extensive library of supported applications and games

## Future Directions
As the technology evolves, future iterations of the PCVR capsule will focus on enhancing comfort, improving battery life, and expanding the compatibility with more devices and platforms. Research will continue to explore new avenues for immersive experiences and user interaction.
