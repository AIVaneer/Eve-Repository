## The Problem

Every play-to-earn project dies the same way:
1. Project launches token
2. Players earn token for free through gameplay
3. Players sell immediately — nothing else to do with it
4. Sell pressure drives price down
5. Lower price = lower rewards = players leave
6. Dead community = dead project

This is called **debasement** — giving out tokens that bring in no backing. Staking rewards, liquidity incentives, gameplay rewards — all add sell pressure with no buy pressure. The math always catches up.

**Examples:** Axie Infinity (SLP peaked at $0.40, crashed to fractions of a cent), StepN (GMT cratered when earnings slowed).

---

## The Solution — The Loop

The token must be the **currency of the ecosystem**, not just the reward.

**Earn → Hold → Spend → Buy → Earn**

- **Earn** — Players earn PCVR Coin through gameplay (level completion, boss defeats, milestones, challenges, PvP wins, mining pool sessions)
- **Hold** — Access tiers reward holding (Bronze 500, Silver 2000, Gold 5000, Diamond 15000, Elite 50000 PCVR). Holding unlocks beta access, governance voting, dev Q&A, custom titles, priority features
- **Spend** — In-game store (cosmetics, skins, effects, gameplay boosts), tournament entry fees, premium game modes, early access to new games. Every purchase burns a percentage (10-35%) permanently
- **Buy** — Players who spent their tokens need more → buy on DEX → price goes up → cycle continues

If any link breaks, the token dies. All four must work.

## The 3-Month Smart Contract Lock

New idea: a time-locked earnings contract.

**How it works:**
- Buyer deposits PCVR Coin into smart contract
- Tokens locked for exactly 90 days — immutable, no early withdrawal
- During lock period, holder earns proportional share of the **Earnings Vault**
- At unlock (Day 90), original tokens + accrued earnings are released
- 7-day gradual release on unlock to prevent cliff dumps
- Option to re-lock for another 90 days

**The Earnings Vault:**
- Funded by REAL ecosystem revenue — not minted tokens
- Sources: store purchases, tournament rake, premium access fees
- Variable APY — tied to actual ecosystem activity
- If no activity, no earnings. Honest and sustainable.

**Why this is different from regular staking:**
- Regular staking: lock → earn printed tokens → sell → token dies
- This model: lock → earn share of real revenue → revenue comes from players spending → spend creates demand → demand supports price

**Key metrics to track:**
- Lock Ratio: target 20-40% of circulating supply locked
- Vault Fill Rate: how fast vault accumulates from activity
- Unlock Calendar: plan events around unlock dates to encourage re-locking

## Anti-Debasement Controls

- Daily emission cap (hard limit on tokens given out per day)
- Per-player daily reward limits and cooldowns (anti-farming)
- Burn on every purchase (10-35% destroyed permanently)
- Earnings Vault funded only by real revenue, never by minting
- Health Ratio monitoring: total spent / total emitted (below 0.5 = danger)
- Server-side validation of all reward events
- Rate limiting and anomaly detection
