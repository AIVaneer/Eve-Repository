## Upcoming Phases

---

**Phase 9 — Unified App Shell** ❌

Build a single app with a game launcher, user accounts, and score tracking. Players open one app to access all PCVR games. Home screen displays available titles and player stats.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 10 — Wallet Integration** ❌

Built-in Cronos wallet for every player. Wallet is created automatically on sign-up. Balance and transaction history visible in-app. No external apps or QR codes needed. Secure on-device key storage.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 11 — Automated Reward Engine** ❌

Backend service that tracks gameplay events and triggers PCVR Coin payouts automatically. Configurable reward rules for level completion, score milestones, and challenge goals. Payout queue with batched settlements. Full transaction logging with blockchain confirmation.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 12 — Mining Pool System** ❌

Multiplayer sessions where combined player activity increases the total reward pool. Proportional distribution based on each player's individual contribution. Real-time pool dashboard showing active players, pool size, contribution percentage, and estimated earnings.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 13 — Security & Anti-Cheat** ❌

Server-side validation of all reward-triggering events. Rate limiting to prevent farming. Anomaly detection for abnormal patterns. Private keys stored in device secure enclave. Smart contract controls including daily payout caps, pause function, and authorized source whitelist.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 14 — App Store Strategy** ❌

Navigate Apple and Google crypto policies. Plan distribution approach. Options include in-app rewards with web-based withdrawal, Android-first launch, Progressive Web App (PWA), or Quest Store native integration for VR.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 15 — Beta Launch** ❌

Closed beta with top community members from Discord. Bug fixes, reward rate balancing, and performance optimization based on real player data. Gather feedback and iterate before public release.

Status: Not Started

---

**Phase 16 — Public Launch & Scaling** ❌

Full public release. Marketing push across social media and crypto communities. Onboard new players. Add new games to the launcher. Seasonal events and leaderboards. Ongoing development driven by community feedback.

Status: Not Started
