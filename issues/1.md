## The Core Problem

Every play-to-earn token dies from debasement — giving out tokens with no backing. Earn → Sell → Price drops → Players leave → Dead project. Axie, StepN, and hundreds of others died this way.

---

## The Solution: The Loop

**Earn → Hold → Spend → Buy → Earn**

| Step | What Happens | Why It Matters |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Earn | Players earn PCVR Coin through gameplay | Creates initial token distribution |
| Hold | Access tiers reward holding (Bronze→Elite) | Reduces sell pressure, locks supply |
| Spend | Store, tournaments, premium, early access | Creates demand, burns supply |
| Buy | Players need more → buy on DEX | Price support, new inflows |

If any link breaks, the token dies. All four must work.

---

## 3-Month Smart Contract Lock

- Deposit PCVR Coin → locked 90 days → earn share of Earnings Vault
- Vault funded by REAL ecosystem revenue (store, tournaments, premium)
- NOT funded by minting new tokens
- Variable APY tied to actual activity
- 7-day gradual release on unlock (anti-dump)
- Target: 20-40% of circulating supply locked

## Key Metrics to Monitor

| Metric | Formula | 🟢 Healthy | 🔴 Danger |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Health Ratio | Spent / Emitted | ≥ 1.0 | < 0.5 |
| Burn Ratio | Burned / Emitted | ≥ 15% | < 5% |
| Lock Ratio | Locked / Circulating | 20-40% | < 10% |
| Net Emission | Emitted - Spent - Burned | Negative | High positive |
| Vault Fill Rate | Daily vault deposits | Growing | Declining |
